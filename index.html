Welcome to Raulet Languages

where language becomes art

I am Axenia Raulet, a polyglot interpreter and translator with over a decade of experience based in the vibrant city of Bristol, UK.

Growing up in Prato, Italy, I have always been fascinated by the power of language and the art of translation.

This passion has driven me to pursue a career in this field, and I have dedicated myself to mastering multiple languages including English, Italian, Russian, Romanian, French, Spanish, and Portuguese.

I pride myself in being a versatile linguist who understands the importance of effective communication between different cultures. I constantly seek opportunities for personal growth and strive to expand my vocabulary and knowledge.

My academic journey includes a Master's Degree in Italian Culture and Language for Foreigners from the University of Bologna and a Bachelor's Degree in Linguistic and Intercultural Mediation from Sapienza University of Rome, both of which have shaped my expertise in this multifaceted field.

Thank you for investing your time to learn more about me, and I eagerly look forward to connecting with you in the near future!
